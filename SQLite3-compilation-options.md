The following [compilation options](https://www.sqlite.org/compile.html) are used in this distribution of sqlite3:
- SQLITE_ENABLE_FTS5
- SQLITE_ENABLE_JSON1
- SQLITE_ENABLE_RTREE
- SQLITE_DEFAULT_CACHE_SIZE=-16000
- SQLITE_DEFAULT_FOREIGN_KEYS=1
- SQLITE_THREADSAFE=0
- SQLITE_USE_URI=1
- SQLITE_MAX_SCHEMA_RETRY=0
